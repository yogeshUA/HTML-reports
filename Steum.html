STEUM

Theory

According to V. D. Allen et al, we have different levels of ability to identify and handle emotions in ourselves and others, based on how we think about ourselves and the world around us. This scale measures the level of emotional intelligence through 37 items. Each item contains a statement describing a situation where respondents have to select an appropriate emotion or the possible reaction to that situation.

Meaning of factor

Emotional Intelligence refers to the ability to identify and manage your own emotions and the emotions of others. It is generally considered to be a key component of success in both personal and professional settings.

Emotional Understandingk

Ability to perceive and understand the emotions of oneself and others.

Emotional Management

Ability to regulate and control their own emotions, as well as the emotions of others.
