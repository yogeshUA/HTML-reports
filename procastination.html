Theory

According to P. Arousal Steel, we have different levels of a tendency to delay or postpone tasks or actions, often despite knowing that doing so may have negative consequences. This scale measures the level of procrastination through 9 items. Each item contains a statement describing the feelings experienced before starting any task. Respondents rate how accurate these feelings and thoughts are, on a four-point scale from Very Seldom or not true of me to Very often true of me.

Meaning of factor

Procrastination is the act of delaying or postponing something, especially out of a tendency to avoid doing tasks that are perceived as unpleasant or tedious.
