Personality

Theory

Based on the Big Five Personality Model, this assessment describes personality in 5 factors, which have been researched and developed over the years and have been the subject of numerous extensive studies in various areas. This assessment measures five factors of personality through 30 items. Each statement describes the thoughts, feelings or behavior one experiences on a daily basis. Respondents rate how often they indulge in the behavior described in each statement on a five-point scale from Never to Every time.
