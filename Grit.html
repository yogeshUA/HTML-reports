Grit

Theory

According to Angela Duckworth, we have different levels of determination and ability to maintain focus and effort over a prolonged period of time. This scale measures the level of grit through 10 items. Each item contains a statement describing the feelings experi-enced before and during challenging circumstances. Respondents rate how accurate these feelings and thoughts are, on a five-point scale from Very much like me to Not at all like me.

Meaning of factor

Grit refers to a person’s passion and perseverance for long-term goals. Grit entails work-ing strenuously toward challenges, maintaining effort and interest over years despite failure, adversity, and plateaus in progress.
