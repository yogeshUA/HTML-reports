Leadership

Theory

According to Peter G. Northouse, we have an inclination toward a specific style of leadership, most natural to us, based on how we think about ourselves and the world. This questionnaire measures three leadership styles through 18 items. Each item contains a statement describing the general feelings about leading others. Respondents rate how accurate these feelings and thoughts are, on a five-point scale from Strongly Disagree to Strongly Agree.

Meaning of factor

This assessment describes the three major leadership styles of an individual

Authoritarian

The Authoritarian style of leadership is one where the leader has complete control over the group and its activities.

Democratic

The Democratic style of leadership is one where the leaders believe in the power of the people.

Laissez-Faire

The Laissez-Faire style of leadership is one where the leader takes a hands-off approach and allows their team to work independently.
