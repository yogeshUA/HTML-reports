Value Priorities
(10 Values)

According to Schwartz theory of basic human values[1], 10 human values are arranged along a circular motivational continuum. Each item consists of a single sentence that describes a particular person in terms of the ambitions, wants, or objectives they value most in life. Respondents rate how similar the person described is to them.

Values Definition

Based on the Schwartz theory of basic values, this assessment describes the four major values of an individual along with 10 sub-values.

[1]Schwartz, S. H. (2021). A Repository of Schwartz Value Scales with Instructions and an Introduction. Online Readings in Psychology and Culture, 2(2). https://doi.org/10.9707/2307-0919.1173
